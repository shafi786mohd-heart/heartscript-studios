The Journey Behind the Script

I do not consider myself a traditional songwriter. I think of myself simply as a person who writes down feelings, trying to give them an impactful vision.

My journey with words started 14 years ago, back in 2012. It began in the wake of a traumatic emotional experience. Finding myself entirely alone with a level of pain I couldn't communicate or share with anyone around me, I turned to pages. I started letting it out. At the time, I didn't think of them as poems or art—they were just my raw emotions trying to find breathing room. It was only the people around me who read them and saw poetry.

Today, through Heartscript Studios, those written feelings have evolved into alternative pop sounds and high-concept AI visual storytelling. But the core mission has never changed. I build these tracks and visuals to align directly with people who have gone through similar deep, emotional experiences. This site and my music exist for one reason: to give you a safe space where you can feel your true self, accept your scars, and stay genuine to your emotions.
