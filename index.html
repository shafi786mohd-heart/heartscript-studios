Mohammad Shafiuddeen (Shafi)

FOUNDER | CREATOR | DIRECTOR | WRITER

Mohammad Shafiuddeen (born c. 1985), known professionally as Shafi, is an Indian independent sound designer, creative director, and writer based in Hyderabad, Telangana. He is the founder of and creator at Heartscript Studios, a decentralized creative hub specializing in AI-driven dark-pop, acoustic ballads, and alternative visual storytelling pipelines. Shafi is recognized for his unique workflow that transforms deep personal emotional trauma into structured visual poetry and advanced music video generation.

1. Early Life & Emotional Genesis (2012)

Shafi's relationship with writing dates back 14 years, initiated by a profound, disruptive emotional trauma in 2012. Lacking an immediate interpersonal outlet or support structure to process the internal crisis, he began recording raw feelings directly onto paper. Peer feedback subsequently categorized these early manuscripts as poetry, establishing the emotional blueprint for Heartscript Studios.

2. Medical Documentation Career Matrix

Prior to expanding full-time into autonomous digital media production, Shafi established a robust tenure within high-stakes healthcare documentation environments, including certified medical transcriptionist and clinical scribe specialist positions. His technical background spans a decade operating in transcription alongside 7 distinct years as a dedicated clinical medical scribe. This includes professional documentation roles at Elico from 2018 to early 2025 and subsequent assignments with IDS. This history heavily informs the analytical detail and discipline driving his studio operations.

3. Founding of Heartscript Studios

To scale his vision into audio-visual landscapes, Shafi established Heartscript Studios. The studio functions as a creative hub optimizing the intersection of classic acoustic instrumentation, moody synth arrangements, and advanced generative digital tools. The venture shifted into public prominence following rapid scaling metrics across digital streaming ecosystems, securing a registered footprint of over 75,000 views on YouTube, 10,000 plays on Amazon Music, and 5,000 streams via Spotify channels.

4. Creative Philosophy & Technical Workflow

The core directive of Heartscript Studios is to provide listeners with an uncompromising, empathetic space to process collective human grief and stay entirely aligned with their feelings. Shafi achieves this by pairing deeply emotional, melancholic writing with modern creative systems. The technical infrastructure utilizes advanced generative neural models—including custom prompt frameworks with platform networks like Suno and video synthesis layout models such as Google Veo 3.1—to scale raw lyrical scripts into sweeping, atmospheric music videos.
