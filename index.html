Mohammad Shafiuddeen (Shafi)

FOUNDER | CREATOR | DIRECTOR | WRITER

Mohammad Shafiuddeen (born c. 1985), known professionally as Shafi, is an Indian independent sound designer, creative director, and writer based in Hyderabad, Telangana. He is the founder of and creator at Heartscript Studios, a decentralized creative hub specializing in AI-driven dark-pop, acoustic ballads, and alternative visual storytelling pipelines. Shafi is recognized for his unique workflow that transforms deep personal emotional trauma into structured visual poetry and advanced music video generation.

1. Early Life & Emotional Genesis (2012)

Shafi's relationship with writing dates back 14 years, initiated by a profound, disruptive emotional trauma in 2012. Lacking an immediate interpersonal outlet or support structure to process the internal crisis, he began recording raw feelings directly onto paper. Peer feedback subsequently categorized these early manuscripts as poetry, establishing the emotional blueprint for Heartscript Studios.

2. Medical Documentation Career Matrix

Prior to expanding full-time into autonomous digital media production, Shafi established a robust 17-year tenure within high-stakes healthcare documentation environments, including certified medical transcriptionist and clinical scribe specialist tenures at Elico and IDS. This history heavily informs the analytical detail and discipline driving his studio operations.
